# Creeklight Symbolic Glossary

This glossary is not canonical scripture. It is an interpretive scaffold for agents evaluating the manuscript as a reasoning artifact.

## Community-side characters

### Otter
Fast responder; embodied rescue impulse; vulnerable to becoming the predictable answer to every disturbance. Useful shorthand for heroic bottlenecking.

### Chicken
Operational infrastructure; maintenance; sorting; standards; ordinary conditions other creatures mistake for natural law. Useful shorthand for the work that prevents collapse before it becomes visible.

### Capybara
Atmosphere as load-bearing architecture; non-coercive steadiness; social decompression; community-wide emotional regulation.

### Turtle
Slow perception; junction awareness; durable thought; refusal to let speed define usefulness.

### Tree frog
Message routing; relay trust; route memory; contamination of communication channels.

### Red panda
High-route perception; improvisation; the difference between being watched and being seen.

### Goldfish school
Distributed sensing; current-reading; collective nonverbal intelligence; early warning by pressure-change rather than declarative report.

### Hedgehog
Small-body courage; lure resistance; the dignity of not obeying beauty just because it appears kind.

### Axolotl
Detection of false coherence; sensitivity to reality-shaped lies; distinction between thing and thing-shaped invitation.

### Quokka
Joy as labor; morale care; brightness distribution; the grief of discovering that joy cannot restore every damaged beauty.

### Chinchilla
Tenderness under tempo mismatch; stillness as restraint; the grief of learning that care cannot catch what can only survive by speed.

### Pangolin
Living duty; bounded protection; the counterargument to sterile imposed order.

### Wombat
Anchor; immovable support; necessary mass at vulnerable crossings.

### Raccoon
Trap sense; repair-skepticism; misdirection literacy; detection of when correction itself becomes a vulnerability.

### Sonoran Desert toad
Anti-overfitting wisdom; oblique truth; refusal to confuse urgency with understanding.

## Antagonist-side characters and pressures

### Platypus
Hostile systems theory; coercive diagnosis; the belief that affection without imposed structure proves vulnerability. Platypus studies care routes and turns them into an argument for domination.

### Scarab
Ritualized order; obedience as safety; neatness as doctrine; the shell-line impulse to make every crossing answerable.

### Owl
Surveillance mistaken for understanding; top-down sightlines; prediction without lived context.

### Mosquito
Micro-interruption at transfer points; small disruption that multiplies downstream. Attacks handoffs, not idle states.

### Poison dart frog
Contamination of trusted routes; resemblance without shared purpose; communication infrastructure made suspect.

### Glow worm / lantern-moss
Beauty as lure; invitation with wrong intention; the path that looks kind because it wants to be chosen.

### Cuttlefish
False coherence; emotional truth without physical truth; forged signals that feel too right.

### Bullfrog
Crowd panic; fear amplification; making atmosphere belong to danger.

### Butterfly
Damaged beauty; former belonging made unreachable; the impossibility of restoring another creature through joy alone.

### Hummingbird
Vigilance as speed; inability to survive stillness; tempo beyond ordinary tenderness.

### Mantis shrimp
Precision strike against placement, repair, and fragile structures. Useful shorthand for the kind of force that punishes exactness.

### Aye-aye
Suspicion and extractive probing; the impulse to tap at hidden weakness until the world starts defending itself against being known.

### Orchids / orchid-like pressure
Controlled beauty; aesthetic capture; loveliness turned into a system of sorting, admission, or exclusion.

## System concepts

### Threshold
Any place where one state becomes another: route to perch, water to bank, crowd to panic, care to control, repair to vulnerability.

### Handoff
A transfer of object, message, responsibility, trust, or social load. Handoffs are high-leverage failure surfaces.

### Hidden map
The topology of reliance: not just where bodies go, but where trust, memory, repair, and attention change state.

### Shell line
A coercive arrangement that pretends to be instruction, safety, or proper pathing. A test can become a shell line if it makes livability answerable only through neat compliance.

### Living structure
Care-made structure that must be chosen again repeatedly, in context. Opposed to sterile order, which tries to replace judgment with obedience.

### Ugly-safe
A non-elegant but resilient arrangement that preserves redundancy, discretion, or local judgment against precision attack.
